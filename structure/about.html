About Us

Our goal

We started this company after hearing the struggles of black devlopers and bussines mans and woamans in the high-tech industy and the walls they faced along the way, this sparked the drive and the idea for this company, our goal is to create a black community that is supportive and pushing to success that helps black people in the the job market, create job and business connections between diffrent people in the community and gives back to the the black community as a whole, and creating an easy path for the generation to come, while still being held by the economy and market laws and being a successful business and company, we belive this way can get to increase the heights of our company capabilities and helping alot more for the black-africans around the world and increasing the success rate of black devlopers and bussines mans and woamans

Our story

The birth of the company started in the year 2022 in the collage of tech-career in the city of lod, israel, when the two founders and the corrent C.E.Os, Chen Semu, a full stack developer and a scientist in biolegy and chemistry, and Bat-el Haiylo, a full stack developer and a highly professional graphic designer, met as a students and had to do a duel Project in thier F.S.D course

The path of actualising our gouls

Our company goal of pushing african people to new hights is being actualised in three ways:

1) creating an open-sorece social midia that is helping black people creating high-tech and business conections that is paving them a way to success

2) a place were people could advice and ask eachother for help

3) creating a place that could by-pass the 'friend brings friend' wall, that kept the industry close knit community, and helps black people find a job, and better yet, helps black business owners to find black developers and help thier fellow community members

4) by sticking to a business model we are creating revenew that is putting back in education and helping the young generation

Aspirations

Our final aspiration is that people of african decent will hold a successful and meaningful positions in thier work place and the industry in-general which will leed to improving the positions of blacks in the socity and to aquality for africans
